MY NAME IS HARRY AND I AM AN ASPIRING WEB DEVELOPER

Where to start.... A good place will be how I stumbled across the web development world. Educationally I didn't train to be a software engineer/web developer. My background was in Sports and Exercise Science and I stumbled across a career as a Business Analyst fresh out of university with Mercedes Benz.

Here is where my passion for technology took over. I became obsessed with supporting business needs in automating processses and product ideation/deliver. During my growth as a BA/Product Owner and had this scratch that needed itching....Web Development/Software Engineering.

As I worked in IT, albeit on the product side rather than development, I kept being asked by friends who had their own businesses whether I could support with a website build or with existing issues with their web platforms. My answer was always "I can support with the analysis and understand what support you will need, but I cannot go end to end". This made me think... What if I could utilise my analytical skillset, and utilise this within web development. This is what became my new obsession, to teach myself how to code so I could support friends and family a like. Skip a couple of years, and a few promotions within the product space, that desire to understand web development still burns deeply, this has lead to me finally taking the leap to become a fully fledged web developer.
